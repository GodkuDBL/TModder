TModder v1.0.0 Changelog
========================

Features:
---------
- Added TModder functionality as an entry point and manager.
- Loads and manages mods found in the Mods directory.
- Compiles mods to .tmo format for use in Terraria.
- Patches mods to integrate them seamlessly into the game.
- Patches the title menu logo with the TModder logo.
- Modifies interface layers to include mod management UI.

Changes:
---------
- more simple and flexible

Fixes:
---------
- compilation errors
